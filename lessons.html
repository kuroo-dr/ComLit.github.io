Lesson 1:
Introductions to Computers and ICT

This lessons covers the definition and types of commputers (hardware/software), their hisstory, and the basics information and Communication Technology and it's role in society.

Lesson 2:
Computer Hardware

Focuses on identifying the major parts of a computer system and their functions, including input and output devices, the CPU, RAM, and storage devices like hard drives.

Lesson 3:

Covers the concepts of system and application software, the purpose of different operating systems (Windows, macOS, etc.), and basic software/hardware interaction.

Lesson 4:
File Management

Teaches essential skills such as creating, saving, organizing, searching, and managing digital files and folders efficiently.

Lesson 6:

Focuses on creating visually engaging presentations using slides, text, and multimedia elements in tools like Microsoft PowerPoint.

Lesson 8:
Spreadsheets and Data Management

Covers the basics of electronic mail (email) etiquette, instant messaging, and using online platforms for communication and collaboration.

Lesson 9:

Addresses threats to computer security such as viruses and phishing, along with how to create strong passwords and protect personal data.
